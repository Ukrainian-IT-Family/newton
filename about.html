about the project
Functional residential complex of business class apartments in the developed area
The concept of the complex is based on technology, mobility and comfort
Each solution of the project is aimed at making your quality of life higher
Location

The residential complex is located in the technological heart - the Nagatino-Sadovniki district. Keeping your historical heritage, in recent years it has been actively modernized and developed, but remains cozy and green.

Innovative technoparks and business centers coexist with park areas and modern spaces for recreation, making the area comfortable and pleasant to live in.

Infrastructure

The territory of the residential complex is a harmonious space that combines functionality and comfort. Closed yard without cars, paths for evening walks and jogging, green areas and stylish public space do NEWTON APARTMENTS cozy and comfortable for life.

Architecture

The residential complex occupies 1.26 hectares and consists of two high-rise high-tech towers. Each tower has own design that takes us to the skyscraper district in Hong Kong.

Futuristic geometry, combination of metal and glass blocks, minimalism and premium materials make NEWTON APARTMENTS a bright embodiment of the style of the future.

Smart House

All apartments have a built-in "Smart Home" system. You are no longer afraid to forget the keys of the house - the entrance to the entrance and the apartment is carried out by FACE ID.

Technological solutions of NEWTON APARTMENTS will solve everyday problems without your participation. Manage communications from your smartphone: you can remotely control the temperature of the air, battery and underfloor heating, turn on and off the lighting.
